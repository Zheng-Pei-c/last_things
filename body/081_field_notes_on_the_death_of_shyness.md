# Field Notes on the Death of Shyness

Observed in adolescence and several literary circles.

Shyness appears to die in some subjects after repeated exposure to disappointment, performance, or public transport. In others it persists for life, adapting into wit, listening, or strategic lateness.

Specimen A reports that shyness died the night she sang karaoke in a city where no one knew her surname. However, traces remain detectable near telephones and compliments.
