# Memo from the Department of Vanishings

**To:** All Temporary Persons, Practices, and Celestial Bodies  
**From:** Acting Clerk for Scheduled Endings  
**Subject:** Reminder

Please be advised that disappearance remains a core organizational process.

However, total elimination is no longer considered best practice. Where possible, staff are encouraged to leave behind one or more of the following:

- a rumor
- a stain
- a revised recipe
- a child with your eyebrows
- a pattern in dust
- a song with two words missing
- an improved silence

Thank you for your cooperation in all matters terminal and cyclical.
