# Fragment in Two Languages

My grandmother said every death is also a rearrangement of address.

Mi abuela decia que toda muerte es tambien un cambio de direccion.

When she died, we found no forwarding note, only her apron and a drawer full of saved string.

Cuando murio, no encontramos nota de reenvio, solo su delantal y un cajon lleno de hilos guardados.

Years later I tie a parcel with one of her old lengths of string and understand that she has not stopped arriving; she has only changed the route.
