# Report on the Star That Resigned

**Archive:** Municipal Observatory, Night Shift Records  
**Object:** Star 11-C, local nickname *Mild Anna*  
**Incident:** voluntary extinction rumor

At 02:13 the star appeared to dim beyond expected variance.

At 02:16 Technician Belen stated, "It has the look of a worker who has seen the memo and decided against Tuesday."

At 02:22 the object brightened briefly, as if reconsidering.

At 02:30 the sky around it seemed larger than before, which is to say that absence had begun its usual public relations campaign.

Three weeks later, the astrophysicists concluded there had been no resignation, only transition: the star had thrown off one form of burning in order to continue in another impossible grammar.

The town, disappointed by the lack of melodrama, held a small parade anyway.
