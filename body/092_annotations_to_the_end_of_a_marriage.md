# Annotations to the End of a Marriage

1. It did not die in one scene, however cinematic that would have been.
2. It died by erosion, then by honesty, then by both parties discovering they were no longer each other's correct weather.
3. At the funeral there were tax forms.
4. Also, unexpectedly, relief.
5. In the years after, each became kinder in different directions.

This should not be mistaken for failure. Some bridges are built to get two people to separate shores alive.
