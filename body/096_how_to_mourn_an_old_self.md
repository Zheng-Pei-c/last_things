# How to Mourn an Old Self

Do not mock the person you used to be.

That person kept you alive with inadequate tools, theatrical opinions, and the best map available at the time. If they were vain, frightened, provincial, or sincere beyond comfort, note that these are common survival strategies.

Light a candle if you like. Keep one jacket. Thank them for crossing the bridge. Then let them stay dead enough for you to continue.
