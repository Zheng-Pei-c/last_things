# Six Ways a City Dies

1. It stops making room for the poor and calls this renewal.
2. It forgets the smell of bread at six in the morning.
3. It replaces all useful gossip with branding.
4. It lights every corner and loses the night.
5. It demolishes a staircase and thereby deletes a love story.
6. It survives all this and still insists it is thriving.
