# Unfinished Ending

At the crucial moment, the ending misplaced itself.

This was awkward for everyone involved, especially the orchestra.

The lovers stood on the platform with their revelations half-ironed. The villain, who had practiced remorse in the mirror, looked suddenly unemployed. Snow, having committed to symbolism, continued falling long past the point of usefulness.

"Should we improvise?" someone whispered.

So they did.

It was, by all accounts, a better conclusion than the planned one. Several plotlines escaped alive. One side character acquired a future. The curtain came down not on finality but on administrative delay, which is the more believable human condition.
