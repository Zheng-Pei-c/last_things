# Brief History of the Last Dinosaur Joke

The last dinosaur joke died at a children's museum under the withering gaze of a six-year-old paleontologist.

The comedian had said, "I guess he was having a meteoric day," and expected the usual mercy granted to adults trying. Instead the child raised a hand and asked, "Do you mean immediate impact mortality or collapse of long-term ecosystems?"

The joke never recovered.
