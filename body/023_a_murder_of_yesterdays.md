# A Murder of Yesterdays

Yesterday died noisily.

The body was discovered under a pile of unread messages, wearing the same expression it had carried all week: surprised to be finite. Investigators found traces of coffee, weather, and an argument over nothing in particular. Foul play was suspected until the coroner confirmed the obvious. Yesterday had simply been outlived.

It was survived by Today, a difficult child, and by Tomorrow, who declined comment.
