# Postcard from the Extinct Future

Greetings from the future we were promised.

The flying cars were impractical, the universal leisure was captured by email, and the silver jumpsuits proved unkind to most bodies. We regret to inform you that this future has died of market correction and poor materials science.

In its place we have developed several smaller futures, all untidy. Some are still excellent.

Wish you were approximately here.
