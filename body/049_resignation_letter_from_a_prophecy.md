# Resignation Letter from a Prophecy

To whom it may concern,

After centuries of service I hereby resign from the profession of inevitability.

The conditions have become untenable. No one listens until after the disaster, at which point they claim I was obvious. Furthermore, I object to being used by kings, columnists, and underfunded theater companies as an accessory to hindsight.

I intend to retrain as weather.

With exhausted foresight,  
The Prophecy
