# The Ghost of the Public Bathhouse

After the bathhouse was turned into luxury apartments, residents complained of steam appearing in hallways where no plumbing should permit memory.

Mirrors fogged with the outlines of strangers. Now and then someone heard laughter in a language not listed in the building brochure. The management called it moisture. The old neighborhood called it objection.

A city can gentrify a building faster than a ghost can update its address.
