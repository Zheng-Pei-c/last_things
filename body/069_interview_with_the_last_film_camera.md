# Interview with the Last Film Camera

**Q:** Are you angry about digital photography?

**A:** No. Anger is for formats with illusions of permanence.

**Q:** Do you miss being necessary?

**A:** Necessity is overrated. It leaves no room for devotion.

**Q:** What killed your era?

**A:** Convenience, abundance, and the democratic catastrophe of immediate review.

**Q:** What remains?

**A:** Waiting. Grain. The elegant terror of not knowing yet.
