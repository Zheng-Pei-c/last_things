# Deposition of a Dead God

**Question:** State your name for the record.

**Answer:** I have had seven names, all badly translated.

**Question:** Are you deceased?

**Answer:** Liturgically. Culturally I retain pockets of activity among grandmothers and gamblers.

**Question:** Cause of death?

**Answer:** Centralization, literacy, and an unfortunate merger with empire.

**Question:** Any final statement?

**Answer:** Gods do not end. We devolve into idiom.
